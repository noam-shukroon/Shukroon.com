Half Life Opposing Force

Half-Life: Opposing Force is an expansion pack for Valve Software's science fiction first-person shooter video game Half-Life.
The game was developed by Gearbox Software and Valve Corporation and published by Sierra Entertainment on November 19, 1999.
Opposing Force returns to the same setting as Half-Life, but instead portrays the events from the perspective of a U.S. Marine,
one of the enemy characters in the original game.

Developers: Gearbox Software + Valve.
Publishers: Sierra Studios + Valve Corporation (digital).
Director: Randy Pitchford.
Producer: Randy Pitchford.
Designer: Rob Heironimus.
Programmer: John Faulkenbury.
Artist: Brian Martel.
Writers: Stephen Bahl, Rob Heironimus, Kristy Junio, Randy Pitchford.

Engine: GoldSrc
Platforms: Microsoft Windows, OS X, Linux
Release Date: November 19, 1999.
Genre: First-person shooter.
Modes: Single-player, multiplayer.
